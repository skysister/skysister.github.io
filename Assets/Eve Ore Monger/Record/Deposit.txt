Deposit Record

Each record represents a deposit into a pool.
It has fields:
- depositID: unique identifier of this deposit
- poolID: which pool this belongs to
- oreType: specific type of ore deposited
- characterName: name of character who deposited
- quantity: how much ore deposited
- estimatedIsk: estimated ISK value given by Eve
- dateTimeDeposited: date and time of deposit
